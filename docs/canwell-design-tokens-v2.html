01
The signature

The concentric circle is the brand, used everywhere.

The thin gold rings in the icon are the most distinctive thing Canwell owns. The system extends them across the site as section markers, trust stamps, divider rules, and frames around hero imagery. Nobody else in the catchment has this.

Typography

Cormorant Garamond for headlines. Especially the italics.

The classical serif anchors the brand and sits naturally next to the wordmark. The italic is the editorial signature, used for emphasis in headlines and as the body of trust stamps.
