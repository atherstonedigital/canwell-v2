### Why visit rather than buy online

**H2:** Why see it in person

**Body:**
Carpet photographs badly. Wool reads cool on a screen. Pile direction changes how light falls. The same colour looks darker on the floor than it does on the wall. The only way to know is to feel it, see it in your kind of light, and put it next to the sofa fabric, the curtain, and the wall colour you're working with.

That's the case for visiting any showroom. The case for visiting Canwell specifically is that we let you take samples home, we don't pretend the cheaper option is fine when it isn't, and the fitters are people we trust to be in your house.

### What we do

**H2:** What blinds do we do

**Body:**
Pretty much every kind. The choice usually comes down to four things: how much light you want to let in, how much you want to be seen from outside, the room (kitchens and bathrooms have different needs from bedrooms), and what fits the rest of the scheme.

The right answer is rarely the one you arrived with. Come in and we'll talk it through.

---

### The types

**H2:** The types, in plain English

**Roman blinds**
Soft, fabric, folds into pleats when raised. Best for living rooms, bedrooms, and anywhere you want softness rather than utility. Made-to-measure in any fabric we stock.

**Roller blinds**
Simple, clean, modern. Best for kitchens, bathrooms, and rooms where you want light control without fuss. Comes in plain, patterned, blackout, and moisture-resistant.

**Wooden Venetian blinds**
Real wood or wood-effect, with adjustable slats. Best for studies, dressing rooms, and bay windows. Warm and timeless.

**Aluminium Venetian blinds**
Slim, modern, low-profile. Best for offices, kitchens, and minimal schemes.

**Perfect-fit blinds**
Frame-mounted, no drilling. Best for tilt-and-turn windows, conservatories, and rented properties.

**Vertical blinds**
Floor-to-ceiling slats. Best for very tall windows, sliding doors, and patio doors.
