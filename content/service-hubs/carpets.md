why_h2: "Why see it in person"
why_body: |
  Carpet photographs badly. Wool reads cool on a screen. Pile direction changes how light falls. The same colour looks darker on the floor than it does on the wall. The only way to know is to feel it, see it in your kind of light, and put it next to the sofa fabric, the curtain, and the wall colour you're working with.

  That's the case for visiting any showroom. The case for visiting Canwell specifically is that we let you take samples home, we don't pretend the cheaper option is fine when it isn't, and the fitters are people we trust to be in your house.
